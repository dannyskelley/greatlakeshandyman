Pet Waste Health Facts:

Dog waste contains harmful bacteria like E. coli and salmonella that can survive in soil for years. Professional pet waste removal protects your family's health, prevents groundwater contamination, eliminates pest attraction, stops unpleasant odors, and prevents lawn damage. Regular cleanup also helps maintain your property value and creates a safer environment for children and pets to play.
